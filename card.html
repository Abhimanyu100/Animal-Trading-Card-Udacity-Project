White tiger

All White tigers have blue eyes. Night vision is 6x better than a humans.

  • Scientific name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Weight: 260 Kg
  • Average lifespan: 12 Years
  • Mostly Found in: MP, Bihar, Bengal, Assam

The White Tiger (also known as the White Bengal Tiger) is a subspecies of Tiger, found throught the Indian subcontinent.
